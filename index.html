About Us

Kumasi Technical University, established in 1954, is a leading institution in Ghana, offering a diverse range of programs in engineering, applied sciences, business, and humanities. With modern facilities and state-of-the-art laboratories, the university provides hands-on training and experiential learning opportunities. Its strong industry partnerships enhance employability and foster a vibrant campus community.
